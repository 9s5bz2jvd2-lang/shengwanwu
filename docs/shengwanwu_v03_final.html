❓ 这是什么What Is This

《生万物论》是一个轻量级研究引擎：把一批学术论文投入"蒸馏漏斗"，从中提炼知识、编织图谱、检测矛盾，最终生发出可证伪的研究假说。 Sheng Wanwu Lun is a lightweight research engine: pour a library of papers into a "distillation funnel," and out come distilled knowledge, a woven graph, surfaced contradictions, and finally falsifiable research hypotheses.

⚙ 流水线The Pipeline · Papers → Distill → Graph → Hypothesize → Rank → Research Questions

从原始文献到可行动的研究问题，整条流水线一气贯通：A single, end-to-end flow turns raw literature into actionable research questions.

🌿 缘起Origin

世间万般学问，散落于千万论文之中。如夜空繁星，各自璀璨，却鲜有人将其连缀成图。 All the world's knowledge lies scattered across millions of papers — like stars in the night sky, each brilliant on its own, yet few have drawn the lines between them.

《生万物论》所求者，非替代人之思考，乃为研究者提供一副"自动望远镜"——从浩瀚文献中，蒸馏知识、编织图谱、发现矛盾、生发假说。 Sheng Wanwu Lun does not seek to replace human thought, but to give the researcher an "automatic telescope" — distilling knowledge, weaving a graph, surfacing contradictions, and generating hypotheses from the vast literature.

一如道家所言：从混沌（原始论文库）中生出秩序（知识图谱），秩序中孕育变化（假说种子），变化中绽放万相（可行动的研究问题）。 As the Daoists say: from chaos (the raw library) order is born (the knowledge graph); within order, change is conceived (hypothesis seeds); and from change, the ten-thousand forms bloom (actionable research questions).

☰ 三生架构The Three-Generations Architecture

道
原始论文库 · 道之混沌 Raw Library — the Chaos of the Dao
arXiv PDF、书籍、数据库——未加工的知识矿藏。如"道"之初，浑然未分。 arXiv PDFs, books, databases — an unrefined ore of knowledge. Like the Dao at its origin: whole, undivided, undifferentiated.
一
蒸馏成库 · 道生一 Distillation — The Dao generates One
将论文全文蒸馏为结构化知识节点（method/finding/concept/limitation），如"一"之始立——从混沌中提取秩序。 Full-text papers are distilled into structured knowledge nodes (method / finding / concept / limitation) — the first "One" arising, order extracted from chaos.
全文提取 · Full-text PyMuPDF trafilatura JSONL
二
图谱成网 · 一生二 Knowledge Graph — One generates Two
知识节点之间，以共享标签自动建立连接（edges），编织成跨论文知识图谱。在图谱中检测六种模式：方法缺失、概念矛盾、跨域桥梁、共享关键词、传递断链、枢纽共现。如"二"之对立——阴与阳，同与异，连接与断裂。 Knowledge nodes are auto-linked by shared tags into edges, weaving a cross-paper knowledge graph. Six patterns are detected: method gaps, concept contradictions, cross-domain bridges, shared keywords, broken transitive links, and hub co-occurrence. Like the "Two" of opposition — yin and yang, sameness and difference, connection and rupture.
共享tag→edges 6种检测模式 · 6 patterns v0.2 scanner
三
语义重构 · 二生三 Semantic Reconstruction — Two generates Three
三层语义重构架构，将机械的图谱模式转化为有意义的科学假说： A three-layer reconstruction architecture turns mechanical graph patterns into meaningful scientific hypotheses:
Layer 1 — 图谱模式检测（v0.2）→ 原始假说候选 / Graph pattern detection → raw hypothesis candidates
Layer 2 — 语义上下文组装 → 假说上下文包（蒸馏全文 + 模式语义 + 关联边） / Semantic context assembly → hypothesis context packs (distilled text + pattern semantics + linked edges)
Layer 3 — LLM语义重构 → 结构化研究问题（含实验方案 + 证伪条件） / LLM semantic reconstruction → structured research questions (with experiment plan + falsification conditions)
类型特定prompt · Type-specific prompts 并行分神 · Parallel daemons DeepSeek / Claude Code
万
万物生发 · 三生万物 Generating Infinite Phenomena — Three generates Infinite Phenomena
从去重、排序后的假说池中，精选 Top-N 可行动研究问题。每条假说包含：可证伪声明、实验方案、评估指标、基线对比、预期瓶颈、新颖性/可行性/影响力评分。如"万物"之繁盛——从简单规则中，绽放出无尽的研究可能。 From a deduplicated, ranked pool, the Top-N actionable research questions are selected. Each hypothesis carries a falsifiable claim, an experiment plan, evaluation metrics, baseline comparisons, anticipated bottlenecks, and novelty / feasibility / impact scores. Like the flourishing of the ten-thousand things — from simple rules, endless research possibilities bloom.
去重 · Dedup Grounding check 优先级排序 · Priority rank Top-20

💭 设计哲学Design Philosophy

由库成论法 / From Library to Theory：不依赖预训练知识，一切假说从蒸馏的论文全文中生成。每条假说可追溯到源文献。 No reliance on pretrained knowledge — every hypothesis is generated from distilled full-text papers, and each one is traceable back to its source literature.

六眼旁观 / Six Eyes Watching：模式检测如六只眼睛，分别审视方法缺失、概念矛盾、跨域桥梁、关键词暗连、传递断裂、枢纽汇聚。多视角才能见全局。 Pattern detection works like six eyes — each examining method gaps, concept contradictions, cross-domain bridges, hidden keyword links, broken transitivity, and hub convergence. Only many perspectives reveal the whole.

论证门与验证门 / Gates of Argument and Verification：假说不经论证不成形，不经验证不出口。生成可追溯、可证伪、可验证的候选，不自动证明理论。 No hypothesis takes shape without argument, none ships without verification. The engine produces traceable, falsifiable, verifiable candidates — it does not auto-prove theories.

低功耗之道 / The Way of Low Power：确定性工作交脚本，判断性工作交LLM。人审稿拆小单元。失败时修最小失败层，不整炉重炼。 Deterministic work goes to scripts, judgment to the LLM. Human review is broken into small units. On failure, repair the smallest failing layer — never re-smelt the whole furnace.

🔮 未来展望Future Outlook

v0.4 规划 / v0.4 plan：引入 embedding-based 语义聚类（替代 tag-based），实现更精准的假说去重与主题归并。 Introduce embedding-based semantic clustering (replacing tag-based) for more precise hypothesis dedup and topic merging.

开放假说 / Open Hypotheses：研究者可输入自己的假说，引擎自动在图谱中寻找支持/反对证据。 Researchers can submit their own hypotheses, and the engine will automatically search the graph for supporting and opposing evidence.

回流机制 / Feedback Loop：验证结果回流知识库，形成"蒸馏→假说→实验→回流"的闭环。 Verification results flow back into the knowledge base, closing the loop: distill → hypothesize → experiment → feed back.
